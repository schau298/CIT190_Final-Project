About the iSpy Game

  • The purpose of the game is to try and find the animal on the farm. The picrture of the animal is shown at the top of the page. The goal is to have the least amount of guesses.
  • There is a 20 second timer that starts right away. Once the countdown ends you must start over!
  • Plans are currently NOT in the works to update the game.
