About the Bee Game

  • The purpose of this game is to help the Bee find his way home. Score as many points as possible by avoiding the obstacles.
  • One current problem with this game is that the highschore you get in one round does not save into the next round.
  • Plans are currently not in the works to solve this issue and there are also no plans to update the game.
